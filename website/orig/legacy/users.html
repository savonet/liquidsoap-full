Some Savonet/Liquidsoap users

We know the following netradios to use Liquidsoap in various ways:

  • Art En Sort is an association to promote music in Normandy.
  • BAZE☆FM: an Hungarian Hip-Hop & R'n'B station, declaring 3 years of stable running.
  • Cool Guy Radio a radio broadcasting improvised electronic music from Denver, Colorado.
  • DEADMEDIAFM is a free form, open community streaming radio and podcast from Indonesia.
  • Deliradio is a live web radio located in Rome, with many DJs and speakers from all Italy and many flavours of music.
  • Eargazm is playing a mix of all flavors of rock, scads of jazz, a bit of fusion, a teaspoon of folk and a dash of comedy...
  • Eastanbul Media Üssü, a Turkish radio streaming mostly electronic music.
  • Fréquence 3, the first French webradio, broadcasting current hits, gold, mainly Pop/Rock
  • Geek radio, the one for which Savonet was created
  • Infusio.net is a deep and dub radio, broadcasting free music from netlabels.
  • KUBE, a student run radio at Keele University
  • La Noche del Hombre Lobo, a Spanish Net-radio dedicated to the promotion of music under Creative Commons licenses.
  • Let's Go Zik, a French webradio dedicated to club & underground music (Trance, Techno, Hand's Up, HardStyle, Electro, House, ...) with a lot of live DJ set.
  • LiveATC is streaming live air traffic communications from all over the world – with single and synchronous/combined audio streams.
  • Loudcaster is for people who want to broadcast radio on-line, and people who want to listen and interact with the broadcasters.
  • NIA, a French electronic radio from New-Calédonia
  • Overfitting disco streams all sorts of disco
  • Oxyradio is a French associative netradio playing free/libre music, relying entirely on liquidsoap for all streaming-related tasks
  • Pi-Radio: a netradio and part-time FM broadcaster in Berlin, animated by an open community currently consisting of 30 independent groups
  • Podradio A French netradio broadcasting live streams generated from various podcasts.
  • Pytagor, a French local FM radio station (Adult CHR)
  • Radio Groovalizacion wants to give a voice to the new musical scenes, to create a place where traditions and modernity freely mingle.
  • RadioPi, the radio of the École Centrale Paris
  • Radio Popolare Roma is a free and independent FM radio in Rome, focused on news and culture. It is part of the Popolare Network, an Italian network of radios that share the same principles.
  • Radionomy is a web platform hosting Internet radios, using Liquidsoap to stream more than 5000 radios :)
  • Radiotango Rosario streams 24h a day of more than 10.000 tangos, microprograms, and history, in Spanish language, from Rosario in Argentina.
  • Radiozerozero is a french radio playing DJ mixes.
  • Радио ВКонтакте is a Russian Internet radio station broadcasting a wide range of electronic music from the world's best DJs and producers.
  • Riff a french webradio mainly focused on rock (especially hard rock), with daily live talk shows.
  • Robotstream is a radio automatisation software written by Numeri'zik.
  • Rural Catholic Radio is a “proud (and very satisfied) savonet user”.
  • SecurityCast Radio is a radio that relays various computer security podcasts.
  • Savana Radio is a web-only radio from Sri Lanka, broadcasting Sinhala (Sri Lankan) music.
  • Radio Sing Sing is an independent FM and Internet radio based in St Malo, France, as well as an association organizing live concerts.
  • Substream.at, a radio for music lovers by music lovers, where you are invited to participate!

And many more excellent but unknown projects... Don't hesitate to tell us if you run a Liquidsoap radio so that we can enhance this list!

Seemingly dead radios:

  • Dolebraï streams music under free licenses, e.g. Creative Commons.
  • NerdFiles is a didactic radio program about free software and copyleft issues, based mainly in Barcelona.
  • Orbitel, a Colombian telecom operator, uses Liquidsoap for some of its netradio channels.
  • Radio ART, a polish artistic radio station
  • Radio Inkorrekt was probably the first German netradio using Liquidsoap
  • Virtual DJ used to display a “powered by Liquidsoap” on its site...
